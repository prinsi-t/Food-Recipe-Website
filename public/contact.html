Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I submit a recipe?

You can submit your recipes through our recipe submission form. Navigate to the Recipes page and click on "Submit Recipe" button. Our team reviews all submissions within 3-5 business days.

Can I save my favorite recipes?

Yes! Create a free account to save your favorite recipes, create collections, and access them from any device.

Do you offer cooking classes?

We partner with professional chefs to offer online cooking classes. Check our Events page for upcoming sessions or subscribe to our newsletter for updates.

Are the recipes beginner-friendly?

Absolutely! We have recipes for all skill levels. Each recipe is labeled with a difficulty rating, and you can filter by "Easy" to find beginner-friendly options.
